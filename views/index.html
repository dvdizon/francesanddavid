Frances & David
 

Our Story

Frances and David met in college at UCSD and were part of the same Filipino organization. The first time Frances and David met, David, a 3rd year, gave directions to Frances, a lost 1st year looking for dance practice for their organization's annual production.

Beach outings, spending Frances' meal points, study sessions, movie nights, and summer hangouts allowed their friendship to grow into something more.

Over the years, their relationship has withstood the test of time and distance, never living in the same zip code since David graduated college.

Through numerous phone calls, FaceTiming, and the occasional online gaming, distance was a challenge, but an opportunity to connect at a deeper level.
